# Sychar Parent Portal — Data Processing Consent v1.0

Effective: 2026-04-30
Controller: Sychar Technologies Ltd

## Data we process

- Child's academic records (marks, grades, exam results)
- Attendance records (daily and lesson-level)
- Fee payment status and balance history
- Discipline records (incidents and outcomes)
- School health/clinic records (if shared by the school)
- Parent contact details (phone number or email address)

## Purpose

Providing parents secure, real-time access to their child's school information through the Sychar Parent Portal.

## Legal basis

Consent under Section 30 of the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019.

## Your rights (Kenya DPA 2019)

- **Access** — request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction** — request inaccurate data be corrected
- **Deletion** — request deletion of your data
- **Withdrawal** — withdraw consent at any time without penalty

To exercise any right, contact: privacy@sychar.co.ke

## Data retention

Data is held for as long as you maintain active consent, or until your account is deleted. Withdrawal of consent does not erase historical academic records held by the school.

## Data sharing

Data is shared only with your child's school administration and is never sold to third parties.
